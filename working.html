HOW IT WORKS

Understand how Sakhidhan helps you save, borrow, and grow financially.

1

Register on the Platform

Sign up with your wallet to start your financial journey.

2

Start Saving Regularly

Deposit small amounts regularly to build your creditworthiness.

3

Apply for a Loan

Request a loan based on your savings and credit score.

4

Credit Score Calculation

Your score is based on saving consistency, repayments, and past loans.

Formula: Credit Score = (Savings × 50%) + (Repayments × 40%) + (Loan History × 10%)

5

Loan Disbursement

If approved, funds are transferred to your wallet automatically.

6

Repay Your Loan

Make timely payments to maintain a high credit score.

7

Grow Your Financial Strength

Responsible users enjoy better loan limits and lower interest rates.
